Mission: Impossible - Ghost Protocol

Movie Overview

Mission: Impossible - Ghost Protocol is a 2011 action spy film directed by Brad Bird and starring Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt. The IMF is disbanded, and Hunt and his team must clear their name after being implicated in a bombing at the Kremlin.

Main Cast

  • Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt
  • Jeremy Renner as William Brandt
  • Simon Pegg as Benji Dunn
  • Paula Patton as Jane Carter
  • Michael Nyqvist as Kurt Hendricks

Release Date

Released on December 16, 2011
