Mission: Impossible - Rogue Nation

Movie Overview

Mission: Impossible - Rogue Nation is a 2015 action spy film directed by Christopher McQuarrie and starring Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt. Hunt and his team must take down a secret organization known as the Syndicate, while also being pursued by the CIA.

Main Cast

  • Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt
  • Jeremy Renner as William Brandt
  • Simon Pegg as Benji Dunn
  • Rebecca Ferguson as Ilsa Faust
  • Sean Harris as Solomon Lane

Release Date

Released on July 31, 2015
